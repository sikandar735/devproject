Our Story​​

ABMC, led by industry pioneers with extensive experience in banking, offers a revolutionary digital platform. They provide end-to-end financial product services, leveraging AI technology for seamless customer experiences. ABMC aims to redefine banking, making it as convenient as ordering food online.

Our Mission​​​​

Our mission is to enable every customer in UAE to access finance and banking products that they can put their faith in and rely upon for their daily financial activities through a simple, fast and secure channel of the customer journey.

Our USP​​​​

ABMC aspires to make banking and finance:
SIMPLE: We offer customer-oriented solutions that cater to everyone’s banking and finance needs with an approach that is simple and easy in nature.
SECURE: Your banking and finance details are secure every step of the way. ABMC is developed with the highest security framework and protocols
FAST: With ABMC you can search, choose, apply and fulfil your banking and insurance needs rapidly with a few clicks.

Partner Program

ABMC partners with banks, insurance companies, valuators, brokers to provide digital solutions to customers. Our partnerships render easy integration of products directly with the ABMC platform to create a seamlessly easy customized experience for customers.

We offer instant digital tie-ups to our partners for offering their products on our platform. ABMC gives partners complete and free integration support. Instantly create your fully customizable, tailor-made banking and finance product experience for customers and go live within 2 minutes of the process through our API integration.

Our complete dashboard and partner portal dashboard provides a complete 360-degree view of the business received and status with live MIS and reports.

ABMC is a unique finance and insurance marketplace. We give unbiased advice and make banking and insurance easier through digital tools. Our platform addresses customer pain points and offers a seamless journey for all banking and insurance needs.
